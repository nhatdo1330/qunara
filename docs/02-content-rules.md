
# Qunara Content Rules

## Language

Write so that a curious 15-year-old can understand.

Use:

- Short paragraphs
- Stories
- Everyday examples
- Clear definitions
- Historical context
- Visual explanations

Avoid:

- Academic language without explanation
- Generic AI summaries
- Unsupported quotations
- Sensational claims

## Scientific integrity

Never say:

- Quantum physics proves Buddhism.
- Buddha discovered quantum mechanics.
- Consciousness creates physical reality.
- Entanglement is spiritual communication.
- Meditation controls quantum outcomes.
- Everything is quantum energy.
- Emptiness is the quantum vacuum.

Use language such as:

- Conceptual resonance
- Historical parallel
- Philosophical comparison
- Not scientifically equivalent
- Evidence remains incomplete

## Required structure

Every comparison must contain:

1. What the Buddhist source says
2. Historical context
3. What modern science says
4. Similarities
5. Differences
6. Misconceptions
7. Evidence level
8. Sources
9. Reflection question
10. Practice exercise
