# Qunara Vision

Qunara is a learning and practice community exploring:

- Buddhist philosophy
- Modern science
- Quantum physics
- Consciousness
- Human experience

Qunara does not claim that quantum physics proves Buddhism.

Qunara separates:

- Buddhist canonical sources
- Scientific evidence
- Philosophical comparison
- Personal reflection
- Speculation

Qunara should help visitors:

1. Learn deeply.
2. Understand difficult ideas simply.
3. Explore similarities and differences.
4. Practice awareness and compassion.
5. Share thoughtful reflections with a community.

The emotional experience should be:

- Wonder
- Calm
- Curiosity
- Humility
- Trust
