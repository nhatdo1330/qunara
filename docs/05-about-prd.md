# About Page Product Requirements
# QUNARA ABOUT PAGE PRD

## Purpose

The About page tells the personal story behind Qunara.

It should not feel like a corporate About page or a generic founder biography.

It should feel like a quiet cinematic journey through:

1. A childhood surrounded by Buddhist tradition
2. A long career in information technology
3. Life challenges and the rediscovery of Buddhist wisdom
4. The AI revolution
5. Curiosity about quantum science
6. The creation of Qunara
7. An invitation to explore together

The page should be personal, honest, warm and intellectually responsible.

---

## Core message

The founder did not create Qunara because he already had all the answers.

He created it because he began asking deeper questions.

Qunara does not claim that quantum physics proves Buddhism.

Qunara explores where Buddhist philosophy, science, quantum physics and human experience appear to resonate, where they differ, and what remains unknown.

---

## Founder story

I grew up in a large Vietnamese family with a strong Buddhist tradition.

My grandparents established a place of worship dedicated to Quan Âm, Avalokiteśvara, in Vietnam.

From childhood, Buddhist chanting, ceremonies and scripture were familiar sounds in my life.

However, I did not understand them deeply and did not pay much attention to Buddhism.

At that time, Buddhism was part of my family environment, but it was not yet my personal journey.

As I grew older, I chose a very different direction.

I pursued a career in information technology.

Over more than 25 years, I worked in different roles across the technology industry.

I experienced rapid change, difficult challenges, success, uncertainty and many personal and professional storms.

Technology taught me to value evidence, systems, logic, experimentation and constant learning.

But life also raised questions that technology alone could not answer.

Over time, I began to recognize the value and depth of Buddhist teachings.

Teachings that once seemed distant gradually became meaningful through experience.

I began to understand Buddhism not only as ritual or religion, but as a deep investigation into the mind, suffering, perception, impermanence, attachment and compassion.

More recently, I became deeply involved in the AI revolution.

My work and research in artificial intelligence led me toward questions about information, computation, consciousness and reality.

That curiosity led me toward quantum physics.

As I explored quantum science, I began to notice that Buddhist philosophy and quantum physics sometimes challenge similar everyday assumptions.

They are not the same.

They use different methods.

They address different questions.

But both can encourage us to question simple ideas about permanence, separation, observation and reality.

This discovery made me realize that Buddhism was far deeper than I had previously understood.

I wanted to create a community where people could investigate these ideas together.

That desire became Qunara.

## Required narrative structure

### Scene 1 — The opening question

Headline:

I did not begin with answers.

Supporting line:

I began with questions that followed me from childhood, through technology, and into the unknown.

The hero should create curiosity and emotional calm.

---

### Scene 2 — Growing up with Buddhism

Explain:

- Large Vietnamese Buddhist family
- Grandparents established a Quan Âm place of worship
- Chanting and ceremonies were part of childhood
- Buddhism was familiar but not understood
- The founder was not initially deeply interested

Important:

Do not exaggerate spiritual devotion during childhood.

Do not present the founder as a teacher, monk or Buddhist scholar.

Use honest language.

Suggested quote:

The sounds were familiar long before their meaning became clear.

---

### Scene 3 — A life in technology

Explain:

- The founder chose information technology
- More than 25 years in the field
- Different roles and waves of technological change
- Technology shaped analytical thinking
- Technology could solve systems problems, but not every human problem

Suggested quote:

Technology taught me how systems work. Life made me ask why we suffer within them.

Do not invent company names, job titles or achievements that are not provided.

---

### Scene 4 — Rediscovering Buddhism

Explain:

- Life challenges changed how Buddhist teachings were understood
- Impermanence, attachment and suffering became personally meaningful
- Buddhism became more than ritual
- The founder started listening differently

Suggested quote:

The teachings had not changed. I had.

---

### Scene 5 — AI and quantum curiosity

Explain:

- Participation in the AI revolution
- Interest in information, computation and consciousness
- Curiosity led toward quantum physics
- Quantum concepts challenged everyday intuition
- Certain philosophical resonances with Buddhism became noticeable

Important:

Never say:

- Quantum physics proves Buddhism
- Buddha discovered quantum mechanics
- Consciousness controls quantum particles
- Entanglement is spiritual energy
- Meditation changes quantum outcomes

Use:

- conceptual resonance
- shared questions
- philosophical parallels
- important differences
- open questions

Suggested quote:

Science gave me new questions. Buddhism taught me to examine the mind asking them.

---

### Scene 6 — Why Qunara exists

Explain:

Qunara was created to build a thoughtful community that:

- learns quantum science clearly
- studies Buddhist teachings respectfully
- explores similarities honestly
- identifies important differences
- practices awareness and compassion
- shares questions and personal experiences
- avoids pseudoscience and unsupported claims

Main statement:

Qunara is not an attempt to prove Buddhism through physics.

It is a place to explore what each tradition may teach us when approached with curiosity, evidence and humility.

---

### Scene 7 — Community invitation

Headline:

Let us explore together.

Supporting text:

Qunara welcomes scientists, engineers, Buddhist practitioners, students, meditators and anyone curious about reality.

Visitors do not need to agree.

They need only to bring curiosity, respect and a willingness to learn.

CTA buttons:

- Begin Exploring
- Join the Community
- Read Our Principles

## Visual direction

The page should feel like an interactive documentary.

Use:

- cinematic full-width scenes
- large editorial illustrations
- gentle scroll transitions
- subtle parallax
- quiet typography
- generous spacing
- restrained animation
- strong storytelling hierarchy

Avoid:

- generic Buddha statues
- glowing Buddha artwork
- floating monks
- mystical energy effects
- chakra imagery
- neon quantum atoms
- corporate team photographs
- fake founder portraits

Suggested imagery:

### Childhood

- A modest Vietnamese family shrine
- Incense smoke
- A small temple bell
- Palm leaves
- Warm evening light
- Silhouettes rather than identifiable faces

### Technology

- Code reflected on glass
- Abstract server architecture
- Data flowing through systems
- Human hands working at a keyboard
- Calm, sophisticated, not cyberpunk

### AI and quantum

- Information patterns
- Wave interference
- Star fields
- Abstract probability forms
- Fine golden lines
- No mystical energy beams

### Qunara

- A subtle lotus emerging from a field of stars
- A pathway through darkness toward warm light
- Multiple people represented as quiet silhouettes
- The outside universe and inner awareness visually meeting

## Content rules

Write in the first person for the founder story.

Use “I” when discussing the personal journey.

Use “we” only when discussing the Qunara community.

Do not invent details.

Do not exaggerate.

Do not claim authority as a physicist or Buddhist scholar.

Do not present philosophical parallels as scientific proof.

Keep the English natural, warm and easy to understand.

Prepare the architecture for Vietnamese translation later.

## Success criteria

The page succeeds when visitors feel:

- They understand why Qunara was created
- The founder story feels honest
- The page creates emotional connection
- The scientific position feels responsible
- The Buddhist background feels respectful
- They want to join the journey

The page must not feel like:

- a corporate biography
- a religious conversion story
- a claim that physics proves Buddhism
- generic AI content
- a collection of decorative images
